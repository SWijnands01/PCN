############
General Exam
############

In this screen, you need to fill in the information about the general exam you performed. The information gathered in this screen will help PCN in selecting the correct diagnosis (and therefore the right algorithm). 
   For example: If the "Congenital Abnormalities" button is selected, PCN will choose the algorithms for Syndromic Clubfoot.
Below there is an example of what the screen looks like:


.. image:: images/GeneralExam.JPG
   :scale: 80 %

--------
What do you do?
---------

Check the answers, and change them accordingly. If you are satisfied with your answers, you can click on “Save”.
